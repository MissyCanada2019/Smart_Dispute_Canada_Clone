Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period.

How do I qualify for the low-income plan?

You'll need to provide proof of income or government assistance. We accept Notice of Assessment, Ontario Works, ODSP, or similar documentation.

Do you offer refunds?

For subscriptions, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. Pay-per-document purchases are non-refundable once the document is generated.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, Interac e-Transfer, and PayPal for payment processing.

Is this a substitute for a lawyer?

No, SmartDispute.ai is designed to help with self-representation but is not a substitute for professional legal advice. For complex cases, we recommend consulting with a lawyer.

How accurate are the generated documents?

Our documents are based on current Canadian legal forms and regulations. They include proper formatting and relevant legal citations but should be reviewed before submission.

Have more questions?

Our team is happy to help answer any questions you might have about our pricing or services.
