Teresa's Story: Why I Built SmartDispute.ai

Seven years ago, I was a single mother facing eviction after standing up to an abusive landlord. With no money for a lawyer and no understanding of the Landlord and Tenant Board process, I had to navigate the system alone.

For weeks, I spent sleepless nights researching tenant rights, learning legal terminology, and trying to figure out which forms to file. I was overwhelmed, scared, and felt powerless against a system that seemed designed to confuse everyday people.

At my hearing, the landlord had legal representation. I had my handwritten notes and a folder of misorganized evidence. Despite having a legitimate claim, I nearly lost because I didn't know how to present my case effectively or cite the relevant laws.

By sheer determination and countless hours in the law library, I eventually won my case. But I realized something profound: justice shouldn't depend on having legal expertise or the money to hire someone who does.

That experience sparked the idea for SmartDispute.ai. I envisioned an AI-powered platform that could do for others what I had to learn the hard way‚Äîanalyze documents, identify relevant legal principles, and create professional legal submissions that stand up in court.

Today, SmartDispute.ai is making legal self-advocacy accessible to thousands of Canadians who would otherwise be at a severe disadvantage in our legal system. This isn't just technology‚Äîit's a mission to democratize justice.

How Our Technology Works

Our Core Workflow
  1. Document Upload & OCR Processing

    Upload your files and our system extracts key information automatically using optical character recognition.

  2. AI Classification & Merit Scoring

    Our AI analyzes your case, categorizes the legal issues, and scores your claim's merit based on evidence strength.

  3. Legal Citation & Precedent Matching

    The system matches your situation to relevant Canadian laws and previous cases from CanLII databases.

  4. Document Generation

    Based on your case analysis, we automatically generate proper legal forms with all relevant information included.
