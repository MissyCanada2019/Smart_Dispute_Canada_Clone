About the Creator

SmartDispute.ai Canada was founded by a full-time mother and tireless advocate with first-hand experience fighting for her family's rights within the Canadian legal system. Frustrated by how inaccessible justice can be to everyday people — especially those navigating it alone — she built this platform to level the playing field. Her lived experience fuels every feature of SmartDispute: from AI-powered document scanning to merit-based case scoring to automatic generation of court forms tailored to the right tribunal.

Her goal is simple but ambitious: give power back to the people, starting with knowledge, tools, and access. SmartDispute is more than a web app — it’s a justice engine for self-represented Canadians.
